Libero


The libero is a defensive specialist who plays in the back row. The libero is allowed to wear a different colored jersey than the rest of the team and is not allowed to attack or block.

Responsibilities of a Libero

  1. Defensive Coverage
  2. Serve Receive
  3. Back Row Defense
  4. Transitioning to Front Row
  5. *IMPORTANT* Depending on skill level: middle back defense, serving, and back row attacks are important responsibilities
