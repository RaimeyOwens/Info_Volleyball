Setters

The setter is the player who sets the ball for the outside hitters to attack. The setter is often considered the quarterback of volleyball. The setter must be able to set the ball in a variety of ways and must be able to read the opposing team's defense.

Responisbilities of an Setter

  1. Always Takes Second Contact
  2. Run the offense and better the defense
  3. See the opposing teams defense set up
  4. Defense and Serving
  5. Blocking
  6. *IMPORTANT* Depending on skill level: Attacking and Blocking as well
