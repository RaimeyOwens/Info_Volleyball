Outside Hitters

The outside hitters are the players who attack on the left side of the net. They are know for being very versitile in the frontrow and the backrow. In order to play this postion you need to be able to hit out of system as well as in-system and you must do serve-receive.

Responisbilities of an Outside Hitter

  1. Attack
  2. Serve Receive
  3. Block Opposite (and maybe middles)
  4. Off blocker defense
  5. *IMPORTANT* Depending on skill level: middle back defense, serving, and back row attacks are important responsibilities
